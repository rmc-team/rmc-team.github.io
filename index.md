## RMC

We are RMC, a team of people around the world who have a passion for computing and the skills to research, modify, and customize many parts of the computing experience. We have members who are skilled with all different kinds of computer hardware and software.

Check out our GitHub for projects and documentation, and our Element community for discussion and updates!
