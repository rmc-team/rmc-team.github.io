## Ending development of macOS projects
### 2nd of March 2021 / 02.03.21

Following the posting of [Switching to Linux](https://julian-fairfax.github.io/blog/200121/switching-to-linux) on the 20th of January and the announcment that I was leaving the macOS on unsupported Macs community, I've been thinking of how I can continue to provide support for and fix issues in my macOS projects and I've come to the conclusion that I can't.

I've bought a new computer which I'm going to get soon, one that will run Linux, and I can't continue to develop my macOS projects without macOS. I've really enjoyed being a part of this community and a part of this development, but all good things have to come to an end. Today I'm announcing the end of development of my macOS projects including but not limited to:
- macOS Patcher
- macOS Downloader
- OS X Patcher
- and openinstallmedia

The repos for these projects will be archived, and their documentation has been updated with the known issues they currently have. I thank you for the support you've given me, and I hope you'll join me in my future projects.

Written by Julian Fairfax
