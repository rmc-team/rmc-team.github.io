## Why use Element?
### 25th of December 2020 / 25.12.20

If you saw our latest blog post you're probably wondering why we chose Element for our communication with users (and internal use). The answer is simple: privacy. Other platforms used by communities (and buisnesses) are usually closed source with proprietary standards. Element is a free and open source project that's built on top of the Matrix standard. This means that you can monitor the code of the platform, host your own server, and enjoy a communiation platform that respects your privacy. 

Let us know if you'd like to hear more about privacy on our Element room. We're always happy to get your feedback!
